## Analytics

The Global Salawat Challenge Dashboard is designed to help you track pledges, donations, and participation in the challenge. Hereâ€™s a quick guide to understand each section and use the system efficiently.

## Overview

This section provides key metrics at a glance, displayed in a visually appealing card format. Each card represents a specific metric related to the Global Salawat Challenge:

- **Total Salawat**: Displays the total number of Salawat pledges made. Example: `35,337,436`
- **Total Pledges**: Shows the total number of individual pledges made by participants. Example: `2,754`
- **Total Unique Emails**: Indicates the count of unique participants based on their email addresses. Example: `1,662`
- **Total Donations**: Displays the total amount donated to the challenge. Example: `$3,526.00`
- **Total Countries**: Shows the number of countries from which pledges originated. Example: `50`
- **Total Organizations**: Indicates the number of organizations involved in the challenge. Example: `14`

---

## Daily Charts

- This section includes visual representations of daily data:
    - **Daily Salawat Chart**: This bar chart shows the number of Salawat pledged each day within your selected date range. Hover over the bars to see exact numbers for each day.
    - **Donations Chart**: This chart displays donation amounts over time, broken down by day. Use this to track the daily progression of donations and identify peak donation days.

---

## Recent Data

- Located at the bottom of the dashboard, this section lists the most recent pledges.
- **Recent Pledges Table**:
    - **Columns**:
        - **Name**: Name of the person or group who pledged.
        - **Amount**: Number of Salawat pledged.
        - **Email**: Contact email of the pledge participant.
        - **Country**: Country of origin.
        - **Source**: Where the pledge was made from.
        - **Date**: Date of the pledge.
        - **IP**: The IP address from which the pledge was made.
    - **Actions**:
        - **Delete**: Click this button to remove a pledge entry if necessary.

- **Latest Countries Added**:
    - This section displays a list of the most recently added countries to the system. It helps in tracking which countries have been included for participation in the challenge.
    - **Columns**:
        - **Country Name**: The name of the country that has been added.
        - **Date Added**: The date when the country was added to the system.
    - **Example Entries**:
        - Norway - 9/29/2024
        - Syria - 9/29/2024
        - Liberia - 9/28/2024
        - Nigeria - 9/22/2024
        - Belgium - 9/21/2024
        - Mozambique - 9/18/2024
        - Estonia - 9/18/2024
